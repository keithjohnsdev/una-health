Don’t go overboard during the onboarding process.

UNA FAST is the quickest way to move candidates from application to being hired.

FAST APPLY

In only 60 seconds you can get candidates in your system, quick and pain free. Allow flexibility in customizing your workflows based on specialty, job, facility.

Candidate Automation

Collect candidate data in phases for quick submit to job opportunities. Notifications and triggers keep recruiters, compliance and candidates in the loop at all times, providing transparency during onboarding.

Sync data to your ATS

UNA’s powerful bidirectional API takes all of the candidate data captured in the hiring process and syncs it with the candidate information in your ATS. Can’t get easier then that huh?

Seamless integration with UNA Test and UNA Wallet

UNA Fast is just the beginning. The valuable information captured in the hiring process will automatically carry over into the two other apps, UNA Test and UNA Wallet.

Supercharged job board

The UNA Fast widget can be installed and placed on your job board, helping candidates easily fill out the quick apply. Integration into Jobstream by JobRobotix is easy through the API.
